## Can New Immigrants Open an RRSP?

Yes.

However, RRSP contribution room only starts accumulating **after you file your first Canadian tax return**.

## Example

If you moved to Canada in 2024 and earned income that year, your RRSP contribution room appears in **2025 after filing taxes**.

## Benefits of RRSP for Newcomers

RRSP can help newcomers:

- reduce taxes
- save for retirement
- build long-term wealth
