## RRSP and Canada Child Benefit

The Canada Child Benefit (CCB) is based on **family net income**.

RRSP contributions reduce taxable income, which can increase your CCB payments.

### Example

• Family income: $80,000  
• RRSP contribution: $10,000  
• Adjusted income: $70,000

Lower income can increase CCB eligibility.
