## How RRSP Contribution Room Works

RRSP contribution room is based on **18% of your earned income** from the previous year.

There is also a maximum limit set each year by the federal government.

Unused contribution room carries forward indefinitely.

## Example

Income: $70,000

Contribution room:

18% × 70,000 = $12,600

If you do not contribute this year, the room carries forward.

## How to Check Your RRSP Room

You can find your RRSP contribution room:

- CRA My Account
- Notice of Assessment
- CRA mobile app
